The aim

Did you know that each year, an individual throws on average more than half of his closet? Only a very little amount of clothes is actually recycled. But this has a huge impact on the planet. The clothing production generates 1.2 billion tons of CO2 each year.

So why don't you help at diminishing our impact on earth and start GIVING the pieces you don't want anymore instead of throwing them?

The concept here is simple. Create an online closet with clothes you don't need anymore. Whenever you're insterested in a piece of clothing from a member of the Community, just make a request and let him choose something from yours. Let's start your online closet!
